\section{Op-amp circuits}

\subsection{Open-loop gain}

We set up an open-loop op-amp circuit. Table~\ref{tab:part1a} shows the measured voltages. At the critical positive voltage $V_+ = \qty{1.33}{\volt}$, the negative voltage jumps up from $V_- = \qty{0.7}{\volt}$ to $V_-=\qty{2}{\volt}$.

\begin{table}[H]
    \centering
    \begin{tabular}{cc}
        \hline
        $V_+$ (\unit{\volt}) & $V_-$ (\unit{\volt}) \\
        \hline
        0.5 & 0.7 \\
        1.3 & 0.7 \\
        1.33 & 2.0 \\
        3.3 & 2.0 \\
        \hline
    \end{tabular}
    \caption{Open-loop gain measurements.}
    \label{tab:part1a}
\end{table}
